Resurrecting Comrade Cluck

Description

After their unfortunate PR stunt of uncerimoniously firing their staff, their product was pulled from Whole Foods. Never to be seen again. Now, we have to fend for ourselves and make this product on our own. No worries, we have labored long and hard to create as close to a replica as one can have.

Ingredients

  • Wet Ingredients
    • Water: 2C
    • Soy Sauce: 3T
  • Dry Ingredients
    • Wheat Gluten: 2C
    • Chickpea Flour: 1C
    • Nutritional Yeast: 1/4C
    • Garlic Powder: 1T
    • Onion Powder: 1T

Steps

  1. Preheat Oven to 325℉
  2. Mix All Dry Ingredients Together
  3. Mix Wet Ingredients Into Dry Ingredients
  4. Knead the Dough Until Glutenous
  5. Form the Dough Into a Log or Cylinder Shape
  6. Wrap In Tinfoil and Place on Baking Sheet
  7. Leave in Oven for an Hour and a Half
  8. Remove From the Oven
  9. Let Sit for 10-15 Minutes
  10. Cut the Seitan into Small Pieces
  11. Saute on the Stove Over Medium Heat in Olive Oil Until Browning
  12. Enjoy!
